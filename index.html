About Our Institution

Vishwaniketan's iMEET

Vishwaniketan's Institute of Management Entrepreneurship and Engineering Technology (iMEET) is a premier educational institution committed to excellence in technical education.

Located in the serene environment of Khalapur, Maharashtra, our institution provides a perfect blend of academic excellence and practical learning. With modern facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we prepare students for successful careers in engineering and technology.
