About Our Institution

Vishwaniketan's iMEET

Vishwaniketan's Institute of Management Entrepreneurship and Engineering Technology (iMEET) is a premier educational institution committed to excellence in technical education.

Located in the serene environment of Khalapur, Maharashtra, our institution provides a perfect blend of academic excellence and practical learning. With modern facilities, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections, we prepare students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

Author Instructions

Paper Submission Guidelines

Follow these guidelines carefully when preparing your submission

Paper Length

The Papers like original Article (OA), Review Article (RA), Letters will be Accepted.

Paper Structure

Paper should be structured in:

  • Abstract
  • Introduction
  • Materials and Method
  • Results & Discussion
  • Conclusions
  • References

Submission Process

Paper should be uploaded at Google link Submit Paper

Presentation

The authors of the accepted papers will be invited for oral presentation in the conference.

Publication Information

  • All papers presented will be submitted to journals
  • Additional publication charges apply upon journal acceptance (optional)
  • First top 15 papers will be published free of charges in the associated journal
